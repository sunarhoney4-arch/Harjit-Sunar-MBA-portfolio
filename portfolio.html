Key Projects

FlexLearn — AI-Driven Course Customization Platform

Designed a next-generation EdTech platform utilizing machine learning to customize course curricula based on individual learning pace and career goals. Established the Business Model Canvas (BMC), developed a low-fidelity prototype, and formulated a market entry strategy focusing on Lifetime Value (LTV) optimization (Gartner Hype Cycle, 2024).

Case Summary → FlexLearn is an adaptive e-learning solution that moves beyond static content. The platform uses AI to continuously assess student performance and dynamically adjust the curriculum, resources, and assessment difficulty. It aims to maximize knowledge retention and accelerate professional skill acquisition for corporate training and higher education markets.

GridSmart — Renewable Energy Project Prioritization

Employed SWOT and Critical Path Method (CPM) analyses to create a proposal for a portfolio management framework to prioritize regional renewable energy projects. Proposed success metrics (e.g., Return on Investment, Megawatt-Hours Generated) and governance KPIs tracked via centralized, interactive dashboards (Project Management Institute, 2024).

Case Summary → GridSmart is a strategic initiative aimed at optimizing investment in solar and wind farm development. The proposal outlines a framework for a centralized Project Management Office (PMO) responsible for project selection, resource allocation, and risk mitigation, ensuring all energy infrastructure initiatives align with federal sustainability goals and regional energy demands.
