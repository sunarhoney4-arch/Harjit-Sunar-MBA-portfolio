Selected academic and client projects that demonstrate my ability to combine analytics, strategy, and practical execution.

FlexLearn — AI-Driven Course Customization Platform

EdTech · Product & Go-to-Market Strategy

Designed a next-generation e-learning platform that uses machine-learning models to customize course curricula based on individual learning pace and career goals.

  • Built a complete Business Model Canvas and value proposition.
  • Created a low-fidelity prototype and user-journey mapping.
  • Defined a market-entry strategy focused on Lifetime Value (LTV) optimization and retention.

Outcome: Demonstrated how adaptive content can increase learner engagement and accelerate skill acquisition for corporate training and higher-education partners.

GridSmart — Renewable Energy Project Prioritization

Energy · Project Portfolio & PMO Design

Developed a portfolio-management framework to prioritize regional solar and wind projects using SWOT and Critical Path Method (CPM) analysis.

  • Defined success metrics such as ROI, MW-hours generated, and payback period.
  • Proposed a centralized Project Management Office (PMO) model for project selection, resource allocation, and risk mitigation.
  • Outlined dashboard concepts for executive-level governance using interactive KPIs.

Outcome: Showed how structured portfolio governance can align infrastructure investments with federal sustainability targets and regional energy demand.
